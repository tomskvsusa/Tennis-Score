Rules & timing

Practical tennis timing guidelines for club play. This app does not run a timer — only reminders so you can learn what to expect.

  • Between points — often up to about 25 seconds from the end of the previous point on pro tours (ATP/WTA); recreational play may be more relaxed.
  • Change ends — after every odd game in a set (1–0, 2–1, …) and at the start of a tiebreak; changeover breaks are often up to about 90 seconds.
  • Between sets — commonly up to about 120 seconds (2 minutes) unless the organizer sets otherwise.
  • On transitions — use the break to walk to the other side and get ready to serve or receive.

Exact numbers depend on the competition rules (ITF, national body, local tournament). Confirm with the referee or organizer when it matters.
